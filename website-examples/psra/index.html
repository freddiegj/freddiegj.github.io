The Park Street Residents Association is for local residents to keep in touch and hear about local developments and events. There are annual meet ups, and meetings including an AGM. (See EVENTS). All local residents are invited to participate by contacting the secretary to have their contact details added to our email list.

In the past we have campaigned concerning proposed changes to the Green. We prevented a telcoms mast from being installed on the grass tennis courts. We were successful in a lamppost campaign to have appropriate street lamps installed. We tackle proposed film companies for funds for PSRA, when filming takes place locally causing disruption.

Events

These events are advertised on the PSRA notice board on Park Street across from the Maypole, and on this website.

Winter Dinners: The committee organise an annual Winter Dinner on one side of Christmas or the other - This is held in a local restaurant and the price ( paid by Attendees ) is usually negotiated in advance. All those living locally are invited. They do not need to have a specific membership to PSRA.

Summer Picnics: These provide an opportunity to gather on the green, swap ideas, and meet the neighbours. Everyone contributes a bit of food and drink. All local residents are welcome.

Jesus Green Events: There are a number of Jesus Green Events held annually see: https://jesusgreen.org.uk/news/upcoming-events/
